Rib Platters

These are a Wilson’s specialty. Served with the sauce on the side so you can taste the smoke and spice.

Memphis Baby Back Ribs: Our own dry rub and many hours in our pit give these lean ribs their mouth-watering flavor. Order a full slab or half slab.

St. Louis Ribs Our biggest and meatiest, these authentic St. Louis cut ribs are slow smoked until they are tender, moist and flavorful. Order a full slab or half slab.

Barbeque Platters

Our platters are served with your choice of two sides, plus cornbread, sauce on the side and our homemade pickles.

Eastern Carolina Pulled Pork: Forget everything you thought you knew about flavor. All night in our pit, this moist pork shoulder makes "the other white meat" something else all together.

Texas-Style Beef Brisket: Slow-cooked overnight to be smooth and smoky just like the best of Texas.

Link Sausage Texas-Style: Slow-cooked, smoky links you rarely find outside of Texas.

1/2 Natural Yard Bird: Juicy and flavorful, our slow smoked, free range chicken is a lighter way to enjoy our full-flavor barbeque.

2 & 3 Meat Platters: Like our other platters, but choose your combination of Pulled Pork, Brisket, Sausage, Chicken and Ribs.
